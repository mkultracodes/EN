Who We Are

PeakActivity is a digital strategy and implementation company,
partnering with businesses to accelerate growth through eCommerce,
Digital Marketing, and Technology solutions.

CONTACT US

Why We’re Here

We want to be your most valued, long-term digital partner so we can help you grow your business. And, while we’re more than happy to solve a single business problem for you, our ultimate goal extends well beyond that. Because we’re not here simply to add names to our client list. We’re here because we live, eat, and breathe digital. And when we apply that expertise to your business you’ll not only be well-positioned for success in the short term but in the long term as well.

Born From eCommerce

Many of our leaders come from major retail corporations where they gained invaluable expertise from running and growing multi-billion dollar eCommerce operations. They also learned the challenges that come with big companies, namely, they don’t move quickly, adapt to change well, and get bogged down in outdated ways of working and thinking. So, they decided to build a different kind of company.

While PeakActivity is built on a rock-solid foundation of eCommerce, digital marketing, and technology expertise, the ideas we generate and the actions we take on behalf of our clients occur in an environment filled with entrepreneurial energy, limitless creativity, and a sincere desire to bring rapid change and success to businesses.
